Making revision easy


DeNOTE helps you share notes with other university students to better prepare for exams. Use it anywhere and anytime you want. Ask questions to other students in your course, catch up on missed lectures, and easily find the notes you need, for free.

Features

Study better with DeNOTE


NOTE SHARING

See the best notes from other students of your course and share your own


FOLLOW USERS

Follow other students to get updated when they upload new content and easily access their notes


COMMENTS

Ask and answer questions from other students of your course


VOTING

Bring the best notes to the top by upvoting them, or indicate the less useful ones by downvoting them


DOWNLOAD NOTES

Planning to revise somewhere without internet? Download the notes you want for offline access


SEARCH

Easily find the notes you need without having to scroll through all your notes
